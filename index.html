How Walk With Me works
600 miles, Egypt to Jerusalem — your quick guide
✍
Create your account
Tap “Create your account,” enter your name, choose your group, and pick a 4-digit PIN. No email needed.
⚡
Log miles by doing real things
Browse 150+ activities across Spiritual, Physical, Intellectual, and Social. Tap any activity to log it. Goal: 60 miles per month.
🎯
Complete monthly challenges
Each month (April–November) has a special 30-mile featured challenge. Complete all 8 to earn the Monthly Champion badge and +100 bonus miles.
🗺
Watch the ward walk together
The Group Map shows your whole ward advancing from Egypt toward Jerusalem in real time. Check the Leaderboard for YM vs YW, Youth vs Leaders, and monthly rankings.
✨
Arrive in Jerusalem
Hit 600 miles and the app throws a fireworks celebration plus a printable certificate. Every milestone unlocks a badge along the way.

Big updates — here’s everything that’s new
✨
Forward to Zion — Enoch’s City
Hit 600 miles and a second journey unlocks: walk in Enoch’s footsteps toward the City of Holiness. Five milestones rooted in Moses 7, 8 XP levels from “Seeker of Zion” to “Translated in Spirit,” and an animated title badge that lives on your Dashboard permanently.
🏆
Spectacular Celebrations
Every milestone now fires a full-screen cinematic celebration — dual-layer fireworks (explosions from center + rain from top), staggered crescendo animations, personalized stats, and a scripture relevant to that exact milestone.
🤝
Partner Challenges
Challenge any ward member from the Leaderboard to complete an activity together. Both finish within 7 days and earn bonus miles + a permanent Partnership Badge. 16 cooperative activities designed for two.
👥
Visitor Role
Friends and family from other wards can now join as Visitors — they see the leaderboard, cheer you on, and react to shoutouts without affecting Level Creek’s group miles.
📜
History Tab — Redesigned
Now with a miles-by-month chart, personal stats bar, keyword search, category filters, and expandable monthly summary cards with color-coded entries and journal notes.
