The Big Picture

600 miles. Egypt to Jerusalem.

Our ward is walking — figuratively — the ancient route from Egypt to the promised land. You earn miles by doing real things: reading scriptures, exercising, serving others, learning new skills. Every activity in the app is worth miles.

The goal is 60 miles per month for 10 months. That's a challenging goal that takes one hard activity or a few easier ones. You'll track your personal journey AND watch the whole ward advance together on a shared map.

Getting Started

Create your account in 30 seconds.

No email address. No password reset drama. Just your name and a 4-digit PIN you'll remember.

  • Open the app at the link below
  • Tap "Create your account"
  • Enter your full name
  • Choose your group: Young Men, Young Women, Adult, or Leader
  • Pick any 4-digit PIN — write it down!
  • You're in. Your journey begins immediately.
Pro tip: Add the app to your phone's home screen so it's one tap away every day. On iPhone: Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android: ⋮ → Install App.

Your Dashboard

Everything at a glance.

The Dashboard is your home base. The moment you log in you'll see your total miles, this month's progress, your personal journey map, and your streak — all updating in real time.

  • Your personal mini-map shows exactly where you are between Egypt and Jerusalem
  • Streak tracker shows which months you've hit 60 miles — keep the fire going
  • Milestone badges unlock at Red Sea, Mt. Sinai, Wilderness of Paran, Jordan River, and Jerusalem
  • This month's featured challenge appears as a hype card — one tap to log it
  • Category breakdown shows your miles split across Spiritual, Physical, Intellectual, and Social

Log Miles

Tap an activity. Miles added. Done.

The Log Miles tab is where the journey happens. Browse 150+ activities across four categories, each worth a different number of miles based on difficulty (★ to ★★★). One tap logs it and it's done — each activity can only be logged once.

  • Spiritual — scripture study, temple, testimony, prayer
  • Physical — hiking, running, fitness challenges, healthy habits
  • Intellectual — reading, learning new skills, budgeting, music
  • Social — service, date nights, family time, kindness
Custom goals: Have your own idea? Add it as a Custom Goal — just describe it, get mile value approved by a leader, and log it.

Group Map

The whole ward, walking together.

The Group Map shows our collective journey. Every mile you log contributes to moving the whole ward along the route from Egypt to Jerusalem. Watch waypoints light up as we pass through them together.

  • Egypt → Red Sea (60 mi) → Mt. Sinai (180 mi) → Wilderness of Paran (300 mi) → Jordan River (480 mi) → Jerusalem (600 mi)

Leaderboard

Friendly competition. Five ways to compare.

The Leaderboard isn't just a ranked list — it's five different ways to see how the ward is doing, compete across groups, and cheer each other on.

  • 🏃 Youth — Young Men and Young Women ranked separately with gold/silver/bronze medals for top 3
  • ★ Leaders — Adults and Leaders with their own ranking
  • ⚡ vs 🌸 — Young Men vs Young Women comparing average miles per month, category breakdowns, and more
  • Youth vs Adults — see how the youth stack up against the leaders
  • This Month — everyone re-ranked by current-month miles only (resets every month!)
🔥 Active this week appears next to names of anyone who logged in the last 7 days. ✨ marks everyone who reached Jerusalem.
